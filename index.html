Lead Pastor Mike Skahan 

Mike and Robin led the charge to birth Epic Life. Their hearts are for the people of Terrell to discover the “life they were created to live” understanding that everyone misses it at times, yet God’s call and purpose still remain. Mike is the Founder and Lead Pastor of Epic Life with an Associates Degree in Christian Psychology and a Bachelors Degree in Christian Education. Robin works as the church Administrator making sure everything runs smoothly. They have two grown sons and two grandchildren. They enjoy spending time with the grandchildren, riding their Harley and doing Crossfit together.
